Research interests

My destiny is to work in number theory, even though I have been exploring different subjects hitherto. In particular, I worked on logic, focusing on positive and monotone fragments of first-order logic and linear temporal logic over finite words. I also worked on conformal nets, especially on the algebraic framework of algebraic conformal nets, defects, sectors and intertwiners.
